Saying Goodbye

February 1, 2011 By admin Leave a Comment

Dear Annie, I would like your advice on letting someone know that they are not a match when you have met them through computer dating. Brad

Dear Brad, If you have not progressed from email to a phone call, it’s fine to end your correspondence with an email. Tell her that she has qualities—and name a couple of them—that many men will appreciate and that you have enjoyed getting to know her. Then say that you don’t feel that you could be a romantic match.
